How this website works?

We are developing several resources to help you keep in touch with your aircraft performance and documentation.

This website does not have a launch date yet, since I am still working on this project.

When navigating through this site, you can:
  • Calculate the total flight time

  • Calculate the fuel burned

Next features

  • Presentation time from flight crew

  • Spredsheet generator to Pilot and Airplane logbook

  • Fuel conversion

  • Weight conversion

  • Weather report (METAR/TAF)

  • Satellite images for weather observation

  • Aerodrome information
